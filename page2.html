Hunger is a state in which a person, for a sustained period, is unable to eat sufficient food to meet basic nutritional needs. This problem is usually common among developing nations, the African continent is one of the place where this problem is really common. It is usually caused by famine, drought and last the displacement of people. Programs such us the world Food program and US Aid helps with relief efforts but the awareness of individual and help in food of charitable donations can help reduce or eradicate this challenge, which makes the earth a little less than a better place.
